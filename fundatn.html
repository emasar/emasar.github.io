Fundation

Our Fundation course is three months programm that covers basic information on electrical engineering and ICT materials, devices, equipment and installation. Maintenance, protection and safety practices.

Aim of the course

To expose students to principal knowledge and techniques involve in the domestic, commercial and industrial electrical and ICT installation.

To indentify the right materials ,devices,and equipment required for a particular installation.

To professionally design and implement electrical and ICT network installations and maintenance with the right protecting system.

To work incondance with ethical safety rules and regulations.

Learning out come for the course

1. Students should be familiar with various types of electrical engineering and ICT system installations.

2. Students should be able to differentiate and select various materials for unique installation.

3. Students should possess the right information and basic technical skill & knowledge of the electrical and ICT installations.

4. Student should be able to design and implement simple electrical and ICT projects.

5. To troubleshoot, restore and carry out maintenance on the installation.

6. Students shoule be safty minded, observe and obey all safety rule and regulations

Model of delivery

Teachning:(theory/practical) and course work.

At the end of this course, student will gain basic knowledge and skills required for average electrical and ICT installations.
