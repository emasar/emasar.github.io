Advance

Our advance installation course is three months programm that provide opportunity for the Students to choose field of specialization on industrial electrical or ICT/CCTV camera installation.

Aim of the course

To allow student choose the field they preffer to work on, concentrate to gain the advance skills, knowledge, training and hand on practical work applicable to real life installations.

To counsel and careful guide the students into their technically gifted area of specialization base on their course work performance.

To help the students focus on their technical strenght and passion. Develope the skills, become the professional and master of the choose field of course.

Advance Electrical Installation

The advance electrical installation focous more on the commercial/industrial electrical installation:

1.Industrial electrical conduit and surface wiring system (panel,cable mesh and ladder,galvanize piping installation).

2.Community electricification project installation( street lighing, poles and distribution line/LV installation).

3. Earthing ,spark light and thunder arrestor and electrical protecting system installation.

4. Air-conditioning system installation and maintenace ( assembling, Troubleshootings, repair).

5.Inverter and solar panel installation and maintenance.

Advance CCTV Camera Installation

The advance CCTV camera installation focous more on the digital/analog CCTV Camera installation and network Infrastructure:

1.Large and small scale CCTV camera system design and installations.

2.CCTV camera installation, Configuration and mobile remote view setup.

3. CCTV camera server and storage setup.

4. CCTV camera Infrastructure ( optic fibre, power and speed cable, kits faberication, poles, mashalin boxes, ICT cabinet/rack installation ,links and networking).

5. CCTV camera fault troubleshootings, repair and maintenance.

6.Fire alarm system installation,troubleshootings and maintenance.

Model of delivery

Indept practical approache and course work.

At the end of this course, student will gain advance knowledge and skills required for both domestic ,commercial/industrial CCTV camera and electrical installations.
